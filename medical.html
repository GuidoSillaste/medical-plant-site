Commonly used medical plants

Ephedra

Ephedra herb was used extensively for food and medicine around the world. The fruit, buds and stem are best eaten raw and have a very mild sweet taste. Very valuable in the treatment of asthma and many other respiratory complaints.

Learn more

Wild Garlic

Garlic is used as medical plant around the world. The whole plant can be eaten and contains a lot of vitamins and mineral. Garlic is widely known for its antibacterial, antibiotic and possibly antiviral properties.

Learn more

Dandelion

The whole Dandelion plant is used as an herbal remedy internally and externally. Dandelion is eatable and very nutritious, having more vitamins and minerals than most vegetables. Dandelion is said to strengthen the kidneys.

Learn more

Wild Ginseng

The whole plant has been used as medicine around the world. Ginseng is eatable and the roots and fruits are especially precious. Helps the body adapt to mental and emotional stress, fatigue, heat and cold.
